### An Interactive Shopping Web Portal for Footwear and Bag Products

STEPS & CARRY is a modern MERN Stack e-commerce platform developed to provide a seamless online shopping experience for footwear and bag products. The platform combines an attractive user interface with powerful backend functionality, enabling customers to browse products, apply smart filters, manage orders, make secure payments, and interact with an AI-powered shopping assistant.

---

## рџљЂ Project Overview

The platform is designed to bridge the gap between traditional retail shopping and modern digital commerce. Users can discover products through advanced filtering options, view detailed product information, manage their cart and wishlist, complete purchases using digital payment methods, and track orders through an intuitive dashboard.

The system also includes a comprehensive admin panel for managing products, customers, inventory, orders, and business operations efficiently.
